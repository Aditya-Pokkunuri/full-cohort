# Student Review System Guide

Welcome to the **My Review** dashboard! This guide explains how to use the review system to track your performance, receive feedback, and assess your own skills.

## Overview

The **My Review** page is your central hub for performance tracking. It allows you to:
- View scores and feedback for your completed tasks.
- Perform self-assessments on Soft Skills and Development Skills.
- See how the organization (Tutors and Mentors) rates your skills.
- Track your progress on a Weekly or Monthly basis.

---

## 1. Navigation and Filters

At the top of the page, you will find controls to filter the data you see:

*   **Period Toggle**: Switch between **Weekly** and **Monthly** views.
    *   *Weekly*: Shows reviews and assessments for a specific week.
    *   *Monthly*: Shows an overview for the entire month.
*   **Date Navigator**: Use the `<` and `>` arrows to move between previous and current weeks/months.
    *   *Note*: You cannot navigate into the future.

---

## 2. Task Scores (The "Score" Tab)

By default, the **Score** tab is selected. This section lists all the tasks you have submitted that have been reviewed within the selected period.

### Understanding the List
*   **Task**: The name of the assignment or project.
*   **Given By**: Indicates who reviewed your work.
    *   **Tutor**: Reviews by Executives.
    *   **Mentor**: Reviews by Managers/Team Leads.
*   **Score**: Your score for the task (out of 10).

### Viewing Detailed Feedback
Click on any row in the task list to open a detailed **Review Modal**. This modal contains:
*   **Detailed Score**: Large display of your score.
*   **Review/Feedback**: Written comments from the reviewer about what you did well.
*   **Areas for Improvement**: Specific advice on how to improve your work in the future.

---

## 3. Self-Assessment (The "My Score" Tab)

Click on the **My Score** tab to access the self-assessment interface. Here, you can rate your own performance based on specific traits.

### Categories
You can toggle between two skill categories using the buttons at the top of the card:
*   **Soft Skills**: Traits like Accountability, Curiosity, Communication, etc.
*   **Development Skills**: Technical skills like Frontend, Backend, Databases, etc.

### How to Rate Yourself
1.  **Enable/Disable Traits**: Click on a trait name to potential enable or disable it if it wasn't relevant to the current period (checked items are active).
2.  **Enter Scores**: For each active trait, enter a score from **0 to 10**.
    *   Use the input box to type your score.
    *   Your **Average Score** will update automatically in the large circle on the left.
3.  **Save Assessment**: detailed breakdown of your self-rating. Once you are finished, click the **Save Assessment** button in the top right corner.

> **Fun Feature**: If your average self-assessment score is **7 or higher**, you'll see a celebratory confetti animation!

---

## 4. Organization Assessment (The "Org Score" Tab)

The **Org Score** tab shows you how the organization evaluates your skills for the selected period. This is **Read-Only**.

*   **Comparison**: You can compare these scores with your "My Score" self-assessment to see if your perception aligns with your mentors'.
*   **Override Reasons**: If your Tutor or Mentor has adjusted your self-assessment scores significantly, a **Feedback / Override Reason** box will appear at the top explaining why.
*   **Skill Breakdown**: Similar to the "My Score" tab, you can view the specific ratings for **Soft Skills** and **Development Skills** given by the organization.

---

## Summary of Terms

*   **Soft Skills**: Non-technical skills involving your behavior and thinking process (e.g., *Second-Order Thinking*, *Ambitious*).
*   **Development Skills**: Technical competencies related to coding and engineering (e.g., *React*, *Supabase*).
*   **Tutor**: High-level reviewer (Executive role).
*   **Mentor**: Direct supervisor or reviewer (Manager role).
